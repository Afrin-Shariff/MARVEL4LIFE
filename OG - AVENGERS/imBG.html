MINI SUMMARY OF HIS LIFE

- Real name: Tony Stark, a billionaire industrialist and genius inventor.

- Inherits Stark Industries, a weapons manufacturing company.

- Kidnapped by terrorists and builds the first Iron Man suit to escape.

- Transforms from arms dealer to superhero, vowing to protect the world.

- Founding member of the Avengers.

- Known for his sarcasm, intelligence, and complex morality.

- Sacrifices himself to defeat Thanos in Avengers: Endgame
