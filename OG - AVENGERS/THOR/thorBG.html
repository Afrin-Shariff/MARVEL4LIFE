MINI SUMMARY OF HIS LIFE

- Born in Asgard, son of Odin, destined for greatness.

- Arrogant warrior banished to Earth, learns humility.

- Reclaims worthiness and his hammer, Mjolnir.

- Fights with Avengers, loses family and realm in Ragnarok.

- Battles Thanos, grapples with grief and guilt.

- Joins Guardians of the Galaxy to find new purpose.

HOW DID HE BECOME THE GOD OF THUNDER

Thor was born with incredible strength and the ability to manipulate lightning, thanks to his Asgardian heritage. His journey to become the true God of Thunder began when his impulsiveness and thirst for battle led Odin to strip him of his powers. Cast down to Earth without his hammer, Thor was forced to grow emotionally, learning compassion, responsibility, and wisdom. His worthiness was restored, reuniting him with Mjolnir and solidifying his identity—not just as a warrior, but a protector. His evolving powers, especially post-Ragnarok and Infinity War, highlight his deepening connection to lightning itself, beyond mere weapons.
