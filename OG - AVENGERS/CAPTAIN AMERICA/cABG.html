MINI SUMMARY OF HIS LIFE

- Small kid from Brooklyn becomes a super-soldier.

- Fights in WWII, loses Bucky, crashes into ice.

- Frozen for decades, revived by S.H.I.E.L.D.

- Joins the Avengers, uncovers HYDRA, fights in major battles.

- Time-travels, chooses a quiet life with Peggy.

- Passes his shield to Sam Wilson.

HOW DID HE BECOME CAPTAIN AMERICA

Steve Rogers became Captain America during World War II through a secret U.S. government experiment called Project Rebirth. Though physically weak, Rogers had a brave heart and unshakable morals, which made him the ideal candidate for the Super-Soldier Serum developed by Dr. Abraham Erskine.
